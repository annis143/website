A map

I spend three weeks in New Zealand. Nothing worked out as I planed it, but at the end it turned out to be a wonderful experience. Dont ever plan anything while travleing. The red dots are all the places I have been durring the three weeks.

Bontanical garden

If you pass Hamilton have a short stop to check out the botanical garden there. Hamiltion gardensis definitely one of the most beautiful gardens I ever been. It is seperated into different themes, if you are in to photography definitely go there.

Sunset at Nelson

I didnt stay long in Nelson as I had to catch my flight to Dubai. But we passed by for one day and had a look at the city. Its really nice. and the sunset at the harbour was beautiful!

Able Tasman

I just had three weeks to travle New Zealand. But it was definitely worth to do a three day hiking trip at Able Tasman. Just pure nature around you.

Big tree

The nature in New Zealand is amazing. Its an untoched country. I was so happy about that, that I huged a tree :D

The North of the South Island

We spend some days at traveling across the northern part of the South Island do to the Able Tasman walk. Durring that time we had amazing sunsets. The only thing that was really anoying were the sandflies. One tip, get the expensive sandflie spray and wear long sleves and cover your feet and ankles!
